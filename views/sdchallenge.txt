This React app welcomes users to sign up and have fun 
exploring San Diego through a curated Challenges List. After chosing a challenge and the user gets to the location, they complete the challenge by clicking the 
					Complete button. Their location is verified through 
					geolocation. Each challenge awards points upon completion.
					 Their total score will be displayed on their user 
					profile as well as the Leaderboard.
				<strong>Stack: React, Node, Express, Sequelize, PostgresSQL
